Description

This is a visualization that reaches over a wide variety of common wine, quantified them based on their price and quality (quality is decided professional wine tasters). The purpose of the project is to acculumulate information about many popular wine into one accessible website. The website will make it very easy for readers to come in and find a cheap and tasty wine while learning something about that wine.
Since wine is very popular among all ethicities and all age groups, this will appeal to a lot of people who wants to try wine but just don't know what to get, also great for people who are trying to explore new options. This website will not be a one time use website, users who are satisfied with the wine they found will definitely return.

Design and Sketch

Design 1

Quality vs Year Produced Plot Chart

Scatterplot chart for taste rating by experts vs year produced. Tooltip includes important information like pictures, taste description, variety, and orgin. There will also be a filter by wine type function. The dots will be color-coded based on the type of the wine. Clutter issues will be solved by the zooming function.

Design 2

Wine Taste/Aroma Filtering sunburst

We are going to have a user-interactive sunburst chart. The sunburst will contain different type of wines and their taste/aroma. User- click on the sunburst will trigger scatterplot to only display the wine with specific quality(similar to filter function).
